# 📊 Business Insights 360 | Enterprise Business Intelligence Dashboard

A comprehensive, interactive **Business Intelligence Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze organizational performance across multiple business functions including **Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive Management**.

The dashboard transforms raw business data into meaningful insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, financial analysis, sales trends, profitability analysis, forecast accuracy monitoring, and executive reporting—empowering stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions.

# 📌 Project Overview

Business Insights 360 is an enterprise-level analytics solution designed to provide a unified view of business performance across different departments.

The dashboard consolidates financial, operational, marketing, sales, and supply chain metrics into a single interactive reporting platform. Through advanced analytics and dynamic visualizations, users can monitor KPIs, evaluate profitability, analyze customer and product performance, identify business trends, and support strategic decision-making.

The project demonstrates the practical application of Business Intelligence principles by transforming complex datasets into intuitive dashboards that enable executives and business managers to monitor organizational performance efficiently.

---

# 🛠 Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 **Microsoft Power BI Desktop** – Primary Business Intelligence platform used for dashboard development and visualization.

• 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data extraction, cleaning, transformation, and preparation.

• 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Developed dynamic measures, KPIs, calculated columns, and business logic.

• 🗄 **Data Modeling** – Designed relationships between multiple business tables to enable accurate cross-filtering and reporting.

• 📈 **Data Visualization** – Interactive charts, KPI Cards, Matrix Tables, Scatter Charts, Waterfall Charts, Donut Charts, Trend Analysis, and Slicers.

• 🔄 **Interactive Navigation** – Page navigation using buttons, icons, bookmarks, and drill-through capabilities.

• 📁 **File Format** – Power BI (.pbix).

# ✨ Features & Highlights

## 📍 Business Problem

Organizations generate vast amounts of business data across multiple departments, making it difficult for decision-makers to obtain a centralized view of organizational performance.

Without an integrated reporting system, answering questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which customers contribute the most profit?
- How is Gross Margin changing over time?
- How accurate are sales forecasts?
- Which markets require business attention?
- How is each department performing?

requires manual reporting and extensive data analysis.

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The objective of this dashboard is to provide a centralized Business Intelligence solution that enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor overall business performance.
- Analyze financial health and profitability.
- Track customer and product performance.
- Evaluate regional and market performance.
- Measure forecast accuracy.
- Support strategic business planning.
- Enable data-driven executive decision-making.
